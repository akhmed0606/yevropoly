Our Polycarbonate Products

We supply a focused range of polycarbonate products selected for durability, performance, and suitability for the Canadian climate.

Multiwall Polycarbonate Sheets

  • Lightweight, high-insulation panel systems
  • Available in H-structure and X-structure, depending on load, span, and insulation requirements
  • Manufactured with co-extruded UV protection on one side (standard)
  • Commonly used for greenhouses, roofing, and wall systems
  • Available in multiple thicknesses, structures, and color options

Standard UV protection is designed for long-term outdoor exposure. Enhanced UV protection levels can be supplied upon request.

Solid Polycarbonate Sheets

  • High impact resistance and structural durability
  • Manufactured with co-extruded UV protection on both sides
  • Optical clarity options available
  • Suitable for glazing, protective barriers, and industrial applications
  • Offered in clear and tinted color options
