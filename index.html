Our Polycarbonate Products

All sheets are manufactured using high-quality resin from leading global suppliers (e.g. Bayer Makrolon®).

Multiwall Polycarbonate Sheets

Lightweight, high-insulation panel systems for thermal efficiency.

  • Structure: H-structure and X-structure available
  • UV Protection: Co-extruded on one side (Standard)
  • Application: Greenhouses, Roofing, Wall Systems
  • Options: Multiple thicknesses and colors available
Standard UV protection is designed for long-term outdoor exposure.

Solid Polycarbonate Sheets

High impact resistance and structural durability for glazing.

  • Durability: Virtually unbreakable impact resistance
  • UV Protection: Co-extruded on both sides
  • Application: Glazing, Barriers, Industrial Safety
  • Clarity: High optical clarity (Clear, Bronze, Grey)
Performance varies by thickness. Refer to TDS.

Logistics & Supply to Canada

Clear responsibilities and predictable process. Terms are confirmed in the quotation and commercial documents.

FCA (Free Carrier)

You arrange main freight and import. We deliver goods to the agreed carrier at origin with export docs.

DAP (Delivered to Canada)

We arrange freight to the agreed destination. Buyer handles customs clearance, duties/taxes, and import.

DDP (Delivered Duty Paid)

Available for qualified accounts and specific order conditions. Included costs are defined in the quote.
