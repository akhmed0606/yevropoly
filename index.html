Frequently Asked Questions

Do you publish prices online?

No. Pricing depends on product type, thickness, sheet size, quantity, and packaging requirements. We provide quotes upon request.

What is the difference between multiwall and solid polycarbonate?

Multiwall polycarbonate is lightweight and commonly used for roofing and canopies. Solid polycarbonate offers higher impact resistance and is used for glazing, safety panels, and architectural applications.

Do you offer UV protection?

Yes. UV-protected options are available. Let us know your application and we will recommend the correct specification.

What sheet sizes and thicknesses are available?

Available sizes and thicknesses are listed on this website. Additional options may be available depending on production.

Do you support large-volume orders?
