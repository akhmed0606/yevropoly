Logistics & Supply to Canada

Clear responsibilities and predictable process. Terms confirmed in the quotation and documents.

FCA (Free Carrier)

You arrange main freight and import. We deliver goods to the agreed carrier at origin with export docs.

DAP (Delivered to Canada)

We arrange freight to the agreed destination. Buyer handles customs clearance, duties/taxes, and import.

DDP (Delivered Duty Paid)

Available for qualified accounts and specific order conditions. Included costs defined in the quote.
