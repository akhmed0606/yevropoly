Logistics & Supply to Canada

We offer flexible delivery options depending on your order size, destination, and preferred level of logistics support.

For many Canadian buyers, the simplest option is delivered supply, where pricing and shipment are arranged around your destination to make purchasing more straightforward.

DDP (Delivered Duty Paid)

We arrange delivery with duties and import costs included, based on the order and destination. Final included costs and responsibilities are confirmed in the quote.

DAP (Delivered to Canada)

We arrange freight to the agreed destination. Buyer handles customs clearance, duties/taxes, and import.

FCA (Free Carrier)

You arrange main freight and import. We deliver goods to the agreed carrier at origin with export docs.

CETA duty advantage

Our European-origin polycarbonate sheets qualify for 0% import duty into Canada under CETA, helping buyers access European quality without added duty costs.
