Logistics & Supply to Canada

Clear delivery responsibilities matter for wholesale. These terms are confirmed on the quotation and documents.

FCA (Free Carrier)

You arrange main freight and import. We deliver goods to the agreed carrier at origin with export documentation.

DAP (Delivered to Canada)

We arrange freight to the agreed Canadian destination. Buyer handles customs clearance, duties/taxes, and import requirements.

DDP (Delivered Duty Paid)

Available for qualified accounts and specific order conditions. Included costs and responsibilities are defined in the quote.

1) Confirm specs

Product type, structure, UV configuration, color, and volume.

2) Produce & pack

Consistent production + protective packaging for shipment.

3) Deliver

Shipment coordination + professional commercial documentation support.

FAQ

Short answers. No fluff.

Do you sell retail / single sheets?

We primarily supply B2B volume buyers. If you have a project, contact us and we’ll advise the best route.

Do you provide technical documentation?

Yes. Multiwall and solid TDS are available in the Technical Documentation section above.

What UV protection do you offer?

Standard: multiwall UV on one side; solid UV on both sides. Enhanced UV can be offered for volume orders (confirmed in the quote/TDS).

What delivery terms do you offer?

FCA, DAP (delivered to Canada), and DDP for qualified accounts. Final responsibilities are defined in the quotation and documents.

What information do you need for a quote?

Product type, thickness/structure, color, volume, destination in Canada, and preferred delivery term (FCA/DAP/DDP).
