Material Quality You Can Specify With Confidence

Our sheets are manufactured using 100% virgin Bayer Covestro Makrolon® resin. For buyers, that matters because material quality affects how confidently you can quote, fabricate, resell, or install the product.

We supply material that buyers can specify clearly:

  • Multiwall polycarbonate with H-structure and X-structure options
  • Solid polycarbonate with co-extruded UV protection on both sides
  • Clear and tinted color options depending on thickness and volume

UV Protection by Design

We do not treat UV performance as an afterthought. Our polycarbonate is produced with co-extruded UV protection, meaning the protective layer is built into the sheet rather than simply applied as a surface treatment.

Standard configuration:

  • Multiwall: UV on one side
  • Solid: UV on both sides

Depending on the product and order conditions, enhanced UV options may also be available. For buyers comparing materials, this matters because UV protection affects long-term outdoor performance.

CETA Duty Advantage

Our European-origin polycarbonate sheets qualify for 0% import duty into Canada under CETA, helping Canadian buyers access European supply without added duty costs.

For businesses comparing landed cost, this is an important advantage — especially when combined with product quality, documentation, and delivered supply options.
