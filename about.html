Uncompromising Material Provenance

Trust starts with the raw material. Every single multiwall and solid sheet we import is extruded from 100% virgin Bayer Covestro Makrolon® resin. This guarantees the structural integrity and optical clarity your clients demand.

European Certified Manufacturing

Our European manufacturing partners operate state-of-the-art co-extrusion lines. Our molecularly bonded UV protection outlasts the harsh Canadian climate, backed by comprehensive 10-to-15 Year Written Manufacturer Warranties.

The CETA Tariff Advantage (0% Duty)

Because our products are manufactured in Europe, they enter the Canadian market under the CETA free-trade agreement. Our sheets qualify for 0% import duties, giving you a massive landed-margin advantage over heavily tariffed Asian imports.
